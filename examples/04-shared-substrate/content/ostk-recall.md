# ostk-recall

A local, private memory substrate: a hybrid-retrieval corpus (LanceDB) plus a reified concept
graph and event chain (SQLite). It is the thing every mind in this example shares — one corpus,
one `threads.sqlite`, many users.

The substrate is deliberately *shared*: concepts, threads, and the chain are common ground.
What each mind brings of its own is attention — what it is focused on right now.
